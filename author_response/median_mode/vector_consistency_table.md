# Baseline Robustness Analysis: Vector-level Consistency

## Methodology

- **Datasets**: Census Income, UFRGS, COMPAS, Diabetes, Bank Marketing, Heart Disease
- **Method**: Vanilla + Logistic Regression
- **Seeds**: 0-9 (10 random seeds per dataset, 60 total combinations)
- **Baselines**: Mean (original), Median, Mode
- **Metric**: Cosine Similarity of Interaction Vectors

> **Interpretation**: Values > 0.9 (ideally > 0.95) indicate that the interaction vector
> direction is robust to baseline selection, supporting the reliability of Harsanyi decomposition.
